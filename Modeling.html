Our Workflow Process

We follow a structured, transparent workflow to ensure smooth communication and exceptional quality at every stage.

1. Planning & Requirements

We begin by reviewing your ideas, sketches, CAD files, references, and technical details to clearly define the scope and vision.

2. Initial Modeling & Draft Review

Our artists create an initial model draft for your review. This ensures alignment before moving to advanced detailing.

3. Refinement & Texturing

We refine geometry, add details, textures, materials, lighting setups, and prepare the model for final use.

4. Final Delivery

Models are delivered in your requested formats such as FBX, OBJ, STL, GLB, CAD formats, or animation-ready files.
